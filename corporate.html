Each colour is thoughtfully selected to complement the overall aesthetic while also serving specific purposes on individual pages. Some colours may take on a more dominant role, drawing attention to key elements or calls to action, while others play a supporting role, providing visual hierarchy and cohesion.     Testing Report

    • Our goals for the test report were the following:
      1. Participants find the website visually appealing.
      2. Participants understand from the website what the concept of our platform is and what problems we are trying to solve.
      3. Participants can easily navigate through the website and find the needed information.
      4. Participants would use the website if they experienced these problems.
    • Our testing methods for the test report were the following:
      • We tested on four participants in total. After the first two participants, we adjusted some things based on their feedback. Then we tested the other two particpants.
      • Our test setting was on campus and the participants used one of our laptops and one our phones.
      • Our test protocol was divided in four parts:
        1. Concept Platform
        From this we wanted to learn whether the participants understood the concept of the platform just from reading the website content.
      • The task was: "Read through the about us page and shortly explain what you think this website is for."
        • 2. Navigation Website
        We let the participants browse through the website to understand if they encountered any difficulties navigating or if there were things that did not make sense to them. We gave them 3 tasks:
      • "Go to the page where you can find your personal match and fill in your information."
      • "Go to our Instagram Page."
      • "Go to your Team Profile."
        • 3. Visuals
        After the participants had navigated through the entire website, we asked if the images/fonts/colors in the website were informative and appealing, and if things could be done differently.
      • The task: "Rate the visual appeal of the website from 1 to 5, with 1 being very unappealing and 5 very appealing. "
        • 4. Relevance
        Lastly, we gave some scenario's to the participants. With these scenario’s we asked them if they could indicate whether they would use the website to adress to problem, and if they could explain why they would or why they would not. The scenario's were:
      • "Imagine you are in a soccer team but your team has a different coach every time because there aren’t enough coaches in the club. Would you like to have a permanent coach and would you consider to use a platform like TeamUp to find one?"
      • "Imagine you want to join a sports team, but don’t know where to start searching and how to reach out to sports teams. Would you consider to use a platform such as TeamUp to find teams and connect with them?"

    Context of campaign and promotional activities

    Context of campaign and message:

    We have launched a promotional campaign for our new website, TeamUp. TeamUp is designed to connect team sport enthusiasts who encounter challenges such as difficulty in joining a new team due to lack of connections or knowledge. Additionally, we provide assistance to clubs and teams in finding the missing puzzle pieces to complete their team.

    With TeamUp we envision a world in which everyone’s unique talents would be appreciated and valued. Where our community would be your one-stop destination for finding like-minded individuals and building lasting relationships. Playing team sports becomes more than just a hobby; it would be a transformative experience that brings people together, fosters growth, and creates lifelong memories.

    To promote our services we created a social media campaign on Instagram and Facebook to firstly create awareness among our target audience. Our target audience are Males and Females from The Netherlands between 20 and 25 years old. They are teamsport enthusiasts and are active on social media platforms such as Instagram. Their favorite brands are often sport related brands like Nike, Adidas, Puma and Asics. After raising awareness, our goal is to capture their interest by offering substantial information about TeamUp's values, mission, and vision. In the third week, we transition to the desire phase. Here, we illustrate the practical applications of the site and its inclusivity, focusing on specific sports content. In the final phase of the campaign, our aim is to prompt the audience to take action by visiting our website, in addition to engaging with our social media pages. In general, our aim is to address the target group with a motivational tone of voice, inspiring them to take action and become actively involved. Ultimately, we encourage them to take the decisive step of joining a team or finding their ideal teammate to achieve optimal results.

    Objectives:

    Reach objectives: Since we are starting from scratch with our social media accounts, it is not yet possible to reach a high number of reach and followers. We will need to build this up over the course of the month by sharing our content and engaging with our target audience. According to Lanier (2023) it is also important to optimize your account and provide a professional look and feel. Therefore, we are setting a goal of 500 accounts reached for now and 100 followers. While it may be challenging, it is certainly achievable.

  • Increase brand awareness among the target audience within The Netherlands by reaching 500 accounts on social media within one month, 1st of April.
  • Increase following among the target audience within The Netherlands by reaching 100 followers on Instagram and 50 followers on Facebook by the 1st of April.

    • Affect objectives: Our ultimate goal with this platform is to create an environment where like-minded team sports enthusiasts can support each other and form a community. We aim to make a strong start toward this goal in the first month by establishing the initial framework of the community.

  • By April 1st, we aim to create a community for individuals interested in team sports, providing connections for players and trainers to join teams or for teams to find new members. We want them to feel welcome in any team sport.

    • Response Objectives: To assess the effectiveness of our social media efforts, we aim to measure the number of website clicks. This will provide us with insights into the activity of our target audience. Using this data, we can make adjustments to our strategies in the future.

  • We want our target audience to join this platform and get in contact with each other to help each other. By the 1st of April we hope to have at least 50 website clicks.

    • Media Tactics:

    Since our target audience mostly uses Instagram, and Facebook we want to use those platforms to promote our brand. That way we will hopefully reach the maximum amount of people. We also hope to partner up with local sports clubs in the Netherlands, collaborating on social media to promote our platform to their members. Positioning ourselves as a solution to finding players and trainers.

    Learning Points

    During the execution of our campaign, we faced a few obstacles. One of these obstacles came from the "realness" of the project. In the beginning we found it difficult to post and promote dedicatedly about something that ultimately does not end up on the market. This also made it difficult to actually gain followers. The reason for this could be because we felt comfortable mentioning that this was a school project in our bio, which could turn people off. Another obstacle we encountered was realizing, after a few weeks, that we had made the wrong choice by opting for Facebook instead of TikTok. Despite our hopes of reaching a different segment of the target audience, we fell far short of the envisioned engagement especially on Facebook. After the pitch we adjusted some of our objectives. We added the objective “Increase following among the target audience within The Netherlands by reaching 100 followers on Instagram and 50 followers on Facebook by the 1st of April.”, we did this to also focus on gaining followers and not only reaching accounts.

    Weekly Reflections:

    Reflection Week 1 - Awareness: During the first week, we were really finding our way with posting content and copywriting. It didn't go too badly, and we reached 30 followers and 61 accounts. The first week was mainly about raising awareness, which meant that we had to reach as many accounts as possible. In the future, if we have to start a similar campaign, we will begin sooner with reels for example, because they are what will help us reach a larger audience. Besides that, we need to focus more on Facebook and be more active on that platform instead of only posting the content.

    Reflection Week 2 - Interest: After week 2 we reached 56 followers and 1000+ accounts. We noticed that posting reels works really well to reach accounts, so this is what we wanted to continue with in the following weeks. We were happy with how we were doing at that point because we doubled our followers as well. During this week we focused on posting content that provides information about who we are, what we stand for and what the concept is.

    Reflection Week 3 - Desire: The third week was about desire. This meant that we really needed to be clear about what it is that we are offering. We posted content about different sports specifically to attract the attention of a more niche target audience per post. We did this by continuing posting reels, and once again, they proved to be effective. One reel received 1,192 views, while another received 2,280 views. Thanks to the success of these reels, we reached over 2,500 accounts. However, our follower count remained stagnant at 57.

    Reflection Week 4 - Action: During our last week, we announced the launch of our website and focused more on gaining followers and increasing website clicks. We were really asking the audience to have a look at our website and motivate them to check it out. We noticed that gaining more followers was very challenging in the final week. However, we were fortunate to have gained some followers and ended up with a total of 66. Another goal for this week was to maximize the number of accounts reached, so we prepared some reels for that. Unfortunately, those reels didn't perform as well as the previous ones. While we did reach more accounts, it wasn't as many as we had hoped. In the end, we reached a total of 2,847 accounts.

Therefore, we should focus on each platform separately to determine how to maximize its potential.
